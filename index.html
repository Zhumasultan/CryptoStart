Awesome Facts

Discover some intriguing facts about the cryptocurrency world.

  • May 22, 2010

    The Most Expensive Pizzas Ever

    Laszlo Hanyecz made the first real-world transaction with Bitcoin by purchasing two pizzas for 10,000 BTC, now valued at over $630 million.

  • Untouched Fortune

    Satoshi Nakamoto's Wallet

    The wallet believed to belong to Bitcoin's creator, Satoshi Nakamoto, contains over $30 billion worth of BTC and remains untouched to this day.

  • Borderless Transactions

    Bitcoin as a Global Currency

    Bitcoin operates without borders, allowing transactions to occur globally without the need for intermediaries like banks.

  • Lost Bitcoins

    Over $140 Billion Forever Inaccessible

    It's estimated that about 20% of all bitcoins are permanently lost. That's over $140 billion locked away in wallets whose owners forgot passwords, lost keys, or passed away without sharing access.

CryptoStart Platform

Interactive education about the world of cryptocurrencies.

CryptoStart is an educational web platform designed to teach users about cryptocurrencies, blockchain, DeFi, and NFT technology. The project is built with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, hosted on an AWS EC2 server running Ubuntu 22.04. All site components are modular, fully responsive, and visually styled in a black-gold-white color scheme to reflect a premium digital finance aesthetic.
